Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 31, 2024

This game is an offline application that does not collect or store any personal information from users on behalf of the developer.

The game uses technologies such as Google AdSense and Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect data to provide personalized ads and improve their services. Please note that these advertising services may automatically collect data and use cookies or device identifiers to personalize ads. For more detailed information on how Google handles your data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

The game also contains links to third-party websites through advertisements. We do not control the content or data collection practices of these third-party websites, and we encourage users to review their respective privacy policies for more information on how they handle data.

Game Progress and Responsibility

The game progress is saved locally on your device, which requires the app to access your device's storage. This access is solely for reading and writing game progress data. No personal or sensitive data is accessed or shared by the developer. As a result, the responsibility for managing and safeguarding game progress is entirely up to the player. Please ensure you have adequate backups and security measures in place to protect your game data.
